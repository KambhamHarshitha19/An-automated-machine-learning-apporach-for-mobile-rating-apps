RATING OF MOBILE APP

Ratings of Customers' decisions are greatly influenced by what they read or see online. Reviews by customers demonstrate their knowledge about quality and experience. In the Google Play store, applications' success can be significantly influenced by fake numerical ratings. A positive review may be strongly associated with a high star rating. Nevertheless, the text format of reviews typically differs from the information provided by user star ratings. This study presents an effective machine learning approach for forecasting app ratings on the Google Play Store.
